About the Creator

Life is Strange was created by Dontnod Entertainment, a French video game studio based in Paris. Known for focusing on strong storytelling and emotional, choice-driven gameplay, Dontnod designed the game to feel more like an interactive drama than a typical action title. The studio worked with Square Enix as the publisher, bringing the project to a global audience. Released in 2015, the game stood out for its cinematic style, branching choices, and themes of friendship, loss, and the consequences of time travel.
